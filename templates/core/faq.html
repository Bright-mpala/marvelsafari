Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Bookings & Reservations

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking safari accommodations 3-6 months in advance, especially during peak seasons (June-September).

What's included in my booking?

This depends on the specific package. Always check the booking details and inclusions before confirming.

Safari Travel

Do I need special clothing for safari?

Yes, neutral-colored clothing, comfortable walking shoes, and sun protection are essential for safari activities.

Are safaris safe?

Yes, when guided by professional rangers and following park guidelines, safaris are very safe. Always follow your guide's instructions.

Payments & Cancellations

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary by property and service. Check the specific terms during booking.

Do you offer refunds?

Refunds are processed according to the cancellation policy of each booking. Contact us for assistance.

Health & Safety

Do I need vaccinations for safari travel?

Check with your healthcare provider. Common recommendations include hepatitis A, typhoid, and malaria prophylaxis for some areas.

What about COVID-19 requirements?

Entry requirements vary by country and can change. Check official government websites for current information.

Can't find what you're looking for?

Visit our Help Center or contact us directly.
